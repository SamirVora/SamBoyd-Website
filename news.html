Surfing in Homunga

A report by Sam Boyd and Tom Lee, 22 September 2012.

Conditions here are the best when the wind blows from the west and the swell is coming from the north, north-west or north-east. The waves start at about 1m high and will hold up to just over 3m, performing best when the water is at low or mid tide position and on a falling tide. It is a beach break, both right and left, and the waves are hollow and also fairly fast and powerful. The length is only short and won't often get beyond about 50m, but still a great place to spend a day.
