Learn More About Sam Boyd's...

Sam Boyd

Sam Boyd was born in Bude, Cornwall, 1986 where he learned to surf at the age of 9. Ever since he was a child, his parents used to take him to their local beach and became fascinated in surfing from watching others.

Tom Lee

Tom Lee is one of the co-founders of Sam Boyd's surf academy from back in 2005. He met Sam on one of the beaches in Cornwall. At the time he was backpacking across Europe, trying to ride as many waves as possible.

Surfing At Sam Boyd's

Sam Boyd's surf academy was founded by Sam Boyd and Tom Lee in 2005 on the beaches of Bude Cornwall where he and Tom taught students in the fine art of surfing, and built the academy to what it is today from their hard work and dedication.

Sam then decided to learn to create custom surfboards so that he could give back to the surfing community what they have given to him.
